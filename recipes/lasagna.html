Classic Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian classic, celebrated for its rich layers of flavor and comforting, hearty texture.
This dish features tender pasta sheets nestled between savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and a generous topping of gooey mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
Baked to golden perfection, each slice offers a satisfying blend of tangy tomato, aromatic herbs, and melt-in-your-mouth cheese.
Perfect for family dinners or special gatherings, classic lasagna is a timeless favorite that brings everyone to the table with its irresistible aroma and delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 400g (14 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic; sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet; cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer 3 noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over noodles. Sprinkle 1/3 of the mozzarella and a bit of Parmesan.
  9. Add another layer of sauce, noodles, ricotta, and cheeses. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and remaining cheeses on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley, if desired.
